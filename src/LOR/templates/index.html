What is a letter of recommendation?

A letter of recommendation is a letter written and signed by someone who can vouch for your skill, performance and character traits in relation to a job, position, or admission to a higher education program, such as a masters or doctoral. Recommendation letters are typically emailed directly to a hiring manager.

Students:

Are you applying for a new job or higher education and need a letter of recommendation from your teachers?
Log in or Register to request a recommendation. It's that simple!

Request Tips:

  • Use the Requester Dashboard to submit a new request or check the status of any requests that you have already made.
  • Select a teacher who knows you in a positive way and has knowledge of your skills in relation to the job or position you are seeking
  • Make sure you have the teacher's email address.
  • Be prepared to inlcude all the necessary documentation the teacher may need to write a recommendation: a cover letter, resume, and unofficial transcript is required to be posted with each request.
  • Allow at least 3 weeks for the recommender to write your letter. Remember, teachers are busy and interact with many students, so they need time to gather their thoughts about you in particular.
  • Though not a best practice, you may be asked to write your own recommendation for your teacher to sign, especially if the teacher is very busy or the submission due date is looming.
  • The Additional Information section of the request form can be used to communicate information to the teacher. You may want to note the class you took with the teacher. You may also want to include here other information that the teacher may request.
  • Stay in touch with your teacher once your request is accepted. Keep tabs on your request status.
  • Be proactive. If your initial request is denied, request a recommendation from another teacher.

  • If you change your mind and no longer need a letter you previously requested, please remember to withdraw the request via the dashboard.

Teachers:

A big shout out to all teachers participating in this recommendation portal! Thank you in advance for your time and consideration writing letters of recommendation for your students.

Writing Tips:

  • There are a host of recommendation templates available on the web for reference.
  • Log in/register to access the Writer Dashboard to review your requests for recommendations.
  • Please record your acceptance or denial in the dashboard at your earliest opportunity, which can provide enough time for the student to make a request to a different teacher.
  • The request will contain the student's email address should you need to contact them directly.
  • Should you request additional information from the student, you may ask the student to provide it in the Additional Information section of their request, keeping all documentation together.

  • When you have finished writing a recommendation, please remember to mark the request as completed via the dashboard.
